               5.11.8  Service Brake Power Output
               The service brake power output of an engine driving a propeller is usually taken to be less than the
               maximum continuous brake power output.

               5.11.9 Engine Specification
               Marine diesel engines are specified as follows:
                       (a)    Four-stroke or two-stroke engine
                       (b)  Naturally, turbocharged or mechanically supercharged
                       (c)  Trunk-piston or crosshead type
                       (d)    In-line or 'V' cylinder arrangement
                       (e)   Low speed engine             70   - 150    rev/min
                       (f)    Medium-speed engine    150   -  900   rev/min
                       (g)   High-speed engine           900  -  4000 rev/min
